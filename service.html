SERVICES WE PROVIDE

Fenesta Lifts Pvt. Ltd. is empowered by a team of skilled engineers and expert technicians to provide safe and reliable installation and maintenance services for lifts and elevators.

Installation of New Lifts

With more than a decade of experience, we provide services for the installation of new elevators in both residential and commercial buildings, including hotels, hospitals, shopping malls, and more. Our team of experts does a thorough evaluation of the building plan and existing infrastructure to come up with vertical mobility solutions tailored to meet our client’s requirements.

Maintenance of Existing Lifts

Like all other electronic equipment, lifts and elevators also need proper maintenance to ensure long durability. We keep your elevators up and running with periodic maintenance checks. We give special attention to the safety and reliability of the equipment to ensure smooth functioning of your lifts. Thanks to our regular maintenance services, you can preserve the asset value of your lifts and increase their lifespan.

Modernization of Lifts

Like all mechanical appliances, elevators too become old and obsolete after a certain period. If you want to modernize or upgrade your existing lift to increase its functional value, we can help you find practical solutions tailored to meet your needs. Whether replacing your old elevator with a new one or upgrading the existing lift with new features, we provide stylish and functional offerings for all your modernization and upgradation requirements.

Repair of Lifts

We have a team of expert technicians who provide regular maintenance and repair services for lifts and elevators. If you’re dealing with faulty or non-functional lifts in the building or the elevator has suddenly stopped working, our team can provide immediate assistance.

Consultation for Elevator Installation

Before you decide to install a new elevator, there is much planning that needs to be done. Without proper technical know-how and an expert’s opinion, finding the right elevator for your building could be difficult. We provide consultation services for new lift installation and maintenance tips to keep them running in the long run.
