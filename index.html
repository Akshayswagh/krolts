Who we are

The world is always evolving. Like time itself, technology also moves forward and needs upgrades. Krolts India Elevators & Escalators is committed to providing the latest and state-of-the-art lift and escalator installation and modernization services that are both safe and reliable. With a vast experience of 16 years in the lifts and elevators industry, we give utmost importance to the safety measures in our services.

Based in Pune, Maharashtra, Krolts Elevators & escalators has gained a global reputation in the industry with our commitment to excellence and security. We have a team of highly trained and experienced engineers, technicians and professionals who can assist you with all types of lift and escalator installation services. Be it a residential or commercial property, our vertical mobility solutions are designed to be robust and durable to give our clients the ultimate safety and accessibility. We can guide you with the consultation, design, manufacturing, and installation process of the lifts and escalators.
